Month 1 — Build Your Foundation

Set up all systems before outreach begins.

CRM Setup & Contact Database

Set up HubSpot (free) or Zoho CRM. Import existing customers. Create buyer persona lists by product type.

LinkedIn Company Page Overhaul

Optimize EKC page with product showcase, banner, and 3 posts/week plan. Set up personal LinkedIn for outreach.

Product Collateral Creation

Create professional PDF brochures for CNG, Industrial, and MEGC with technical specs, certifications, and case studies.

Competitor & Market Research

Map top 10 competitors (Luxfer, Worthington, CP Industries). Find pricing gaps and positioning opportunities.

Certifications Audit

List all certifications (ISO 11439, DOT, CE, etc.). Highlight certifications on all marketing material — buyers need this first.

Month 2 — Digital Presence & Directory Listings

Get discovered by buyers searching online.

B2B Marketplace Listings

List on Alibaba (Gold Supplier), TradeIndia, ExportHub, Made-in-China, and IndiaMart with full product specs and certifications.

Industry Directory Registrations

Register on Kompass, Thomasnet, Gas Equipment Directory, NGVA Europe member directory, and CGA (Compressed Gas Association) resources.

Email Campaign Setup

Set up Mailchimp/Brevo. Create a 5-email cold outreach sequence. Write email templates for each product line. Aim for <300 word emails.

Website SEO Audit

Target keywords: "CNG cylinder manufacturer UAE", "industrial cylinder exporter", "MEGC supplier". Optimize product pages accordingly.

Trade Show Calendar Planning

Identify and register for: ADIPEC Abu Dhabi, Gastech, CNG/LNG Americas, Africa Oil Week, Hannover Messe. Book exhibition space early.

Month 3 — Launch Multi-Channel Outreach

Start active lead generation across all channels simultaneously.

LinkedIn Sales Navigator Campaign

Target: Procurement Managers, Fleet Managers, Gas Plant Directors. Send 30–50 personalized connection requests/day. Follow up with value message.

Cold Email Campaigns — 500 Contacts

Send targeted cold emails to CNG fleet operators in South Asia and South America. Personalize by country. Target 25%+ open rate.

Agent & Distributor Identification

Find 2–3 agents per priority country (India, Nigeria, Brazil). Use UAE Export Authority and Chamber of Commerce connections.

Tender & Procurement Portals

Register on UNGM (UN Global Marketplace), DG Market (EU), World Bank e-Procurement, national govt. portals in target markets.

Content Marketing — LinkedIn Articles

Publish 2 technical articles: "How to choose CNG cylinders" and "MEGC vs tube trailers". Position EKC as technical authority.

Month 4 — Trade Shows & Partnership Deals

Maximum face-to-face exposure and agent signing.

ADIPEC / Key Trade Show Attendance

Man the booth. Pre-book 20+ meetings using LinkedIn. Collect 100+ business cards. Follow up within 24 hours via email.

Sign First Agent Agreements

Finalize distribution agreements with 2–3 agents in India, Nigeria, and Brazil. Set quarterly targets and commission structure.

Dubai Exports (UAE Export Authority)

Register with Dubai Exports for market missions, subsidized trade show participation, and buyer-seller matchmaking events.

Warm Follow-up Campaign

Call and email all Month 3 leads. Qualify into Hot/Warm/Cold. Send quotation to Hot leads within 48 hours.

WhatsApp Business Broadcast

Use WhatsApp Business for markets like India, Middle East, Africa. Share product updates, price lists, and quick RFQ links.

Month 5 — Pipeline Nurturing & Proposals

Move hot leads to quotation and order stage.

Custom Proposal Templates

Create professional quotation templates per product. Include payment terms, Incoterms (FOB/CIF Dubai), delivery timelines, and warranty.

Factory Visit Invitations

Invite top 5–10 high-value prospects for a Dubai factory tour. This converts hesitant buyers — trust through transparency.

Customer Testimonials & Case Studies

Collect 3–5 testimonials from existing customers. Write one-page case study for each product showing problems solved and results.

Re-engagement Campaign

Email all Cold leads with a new angle: price update, new certification, or case study. Convert some to Warm.

Mid-Point Performance Review

Review which channels gave best ROI. Double down on top 2 channels. Drop or pause lowest performers. Adjust Month 6 plan.

Month 6 — Scale & Close

Maximize conversions and set up for Year 2.

Close Pilot Orders

Negotiate and close first orders from top 5 leads — even small pilot orders. A first order builds a long-term relationship.

Referral Program Launch

Ask existing customers to refer peers for a commission or volume discount. Industry networks are tight — word-of-mouth is gold.

Tenders Response Submissions

Submit bids for any open government/UN tenders identified in Month 3. Even losing bids builds your tender track record.

Year 2 Planning

Map which 3 new markets to enter in Year 2. Plan trade shows, assign agent targets, and set annual revenue pipeline goal.

LinkedIn Paid Ads — Retargeting

Run targeted LinkedIn ads to website visitors and profile viewers. Small budget ($500/mo) for retargeting decision-makers.

Weekly rhythm — stick to this

Monday & Tuesday — LinkedIn outreach

Send 40–50 connection requests. Follow up on pending conversations. Post 1 content piece.

Wednesday — Email follow-ups

Review email campaign stats. Follow up on opens. Send new batch to cold list.

Thursday — Phone & WhatsApp outreach

Call 10–15 warm leads. WhatsApp broadcast update to prospects in Asia/Africa/Middle East.

Friday — Review & plan

Update CRM. Review week's leads. Qualify pipeline. Plan next week's target list. Send weekly self-report.

Most important metric: Number of qualified conversations started per week. Everything else is vanity. If you are having 15+ real conversations per week, orders will follow.
